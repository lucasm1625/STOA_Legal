Managing Your Subscription

Stoa subscriptions are billed and managed entirely through your Apple ID, not by us directly.

  • To view, change, or cancel: open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Stoa.
  • Free trial: if you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged.
  • Restore a purchase (e.g. after reinstalling): open Stoa → on the subscription screen, tap "Restore purchase."
  • Refunds: these are handled by Apple, not Stoa. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Your Data & Account

Your journal entries, moods, and practice history are stored only on your device — we can't see or restore them from our side. Deleting the app deletes that data permanently.

To delete your account and all local data from within the app: Settings → Delete account. See our Privacy Policy for the full details on what we do and don't store.

About Aurelia

Aurelia is an AI companion for reflection, not a licensed therapist or medical professional. If you're in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or contact your local emergency services — Stoa is not equipped to help in an emergency.

Reporting a Bug or Sending Feedback

Found something broken, or have an idea for Stoa? Email support@stoa.app with:

  • What you were doing when it happened
  • What you expected vs. what actually happened
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version, if it's a display or crash issue

Screenshots always help.
