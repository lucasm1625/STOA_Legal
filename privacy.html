Privacy Policy

Effective June 28, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

The short version: Your journal entries, moods, and practice logs live only on your device. The only data that leaves your device is your conversation with Aurelia, which is sent to our secure server to generate her response and is not stored beyond that request.

1. Who We Are

Stoa is a personal wellness app rooted in Stoic philosophy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App. "We," "us," and "our" refer to the Stoa developer. Questions? Reach us at support@stoa.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect as little as possible. Here is exactly what is collected and where it lives:

Data Where it lives Who can see it
Your name (optional) On your device only Only you
Journal entries On your device only Only you
Mood logs On your device only Only you
Practice logs & streaks On your device only Only you
Chat messages (Aurelia) On your device + our secure proxy server (in transit only) Sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response; not stored by us
Voice audio Processed on-device via Apple Speech Recognition Apple (per their privacy policy); not sent to us
Sign-in identifier Stored securely in your device Keychain Only you; we never see your Apple ID or email password

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking pixels. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 5.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to deliver the App's features:

  • Your name is used to personalize your greeting.
  • Journal entries, moods, and practice logs are used to build Aurelia's memory context — a short summary passed along with each chat message so she can speak to your experience. This summary never leaves your device except when included in a chat request.
  • Chat messages are sent to our proxy server, which forwards them to Anthropic's Claude API to generate Aurelia's reply. The proxy does not log or store message content.
  • Voice input is transcribed on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework and converted to text before being sent anywhere.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data — your journal, moods, streaks, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and Keychain. It is not backed up to our servers.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device. We have no copy of it.

Chat requests to Aurelia are sent over HTTPS to our proxy server. The proxy is a stateless intermediary: it receives your message, forwards it to Anthropic, and returns the response. It does not write any message content to disk or a database.

5. Third-Party Services

Stoa uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Processes chat messages to generate Aurelia's responses. Anthropic may use API inputs to improve safety and reliability per their usage policy. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs — Converts Aurelia's text responses to speech audio when voice mode is enabled. Text is sent to ElevenLabs only when you have voice enabled. See ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — Manages your subscription and payment. Apple's privacy practices govern all payment data. We never receive or store your payment information.
  • Apple Speech Recognition — Used for voice input to Aurelia. Audio is processed by Apple per their framework terms. We do not receive raw audio.

6. Children's Privacy

Stoa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, please contact us at support@stoa.app and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Because your personal data lives on your device, you are always in control:

  • Access and deletion: You can view and delete your journal entries, mood logs, and chat history directly within the App. Deleting the App removes all data permanently.
  • Name: You can change or remove your name at any time in Settings.
  • Voice: You can disable Aurelia's voice output and microphone input at any time in the chat screen.
  • Subscription: You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Because we do not store your personal data on our servers, fulfilling most data subject requests simply means deleting the App from your device. For any other requests, contact support@stoa.app.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain your personal data on our servers. Chat messages exist on our proxy server only for the duration of a single API request — typically under two seconds — and are never written to storage. All other data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with a new effective date. For material changes, we will provide notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@stoa.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
